Our Story

30+ Years of Educational Excellence

Since 1994, Saha Institute of Management & Technology (SIMT) has been a beacon of excellence in professional education, transforming aspirations into achievements.

As an authorized study center of Maharshi Dayanand University, Rohtak, we inspire students who aspire to be real professionals. Our institution was established with the primary objective of providing the highest quality of education through innovative teaching methodologies.

Our dedicated faculty comprises proficient academicians, technocrats, financial experts, and professionals from various fields with proven credentials and rich experience. Through partnerships with numerous organizations, our team has gained practical exposure that helps us visualize the quality and caliber required for future success.

1000+ Students Enrolled

More than a thousand students are currently enrolled across various programs, testament to our commitment to excellence.
