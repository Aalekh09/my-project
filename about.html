Our Story

Saha institute of Management & Technology (SIMT) was an authorized study centre of Maharshi Dayanand University, Rohtak. It is inspired for students who aspire to be real professionals. SIMT was established with the main objective of providing best quality of education. Our dedicated faculty strives to help the students for achieving their aim. SIMT is an amalgamation of proficient Academicians, Technocrats, Financial experts and other professionals from various fields with proven credentials and rich experience. By being with number of organizations and acquired practical exposure, SIMT team has visualized the quality and caliber of future. Our main focus is to provide 100% job assistance to our students. More than thousands students are enrolled under various categories.
